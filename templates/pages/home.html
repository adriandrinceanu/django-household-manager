  • How does it all work?

    Hi, welcome to my Household Manager App where families can manage the family members, chores, budgets and expenses.
    We have two types of users with different permissions: Family Leader and Family Member.

Family Leader
Permissions
  • Can create family and members
  • Can assign chores to family members
  • Can create monthly budgets
  • Can create category budgets
  • Can create expenses

    • First, create an account, create your family and family members. Then, you can assign chores to specific family members, you can create budgets and assign them to different budget categories and then you can create expenses.

Family Member
Permissions
  • Can view family and members
  • Can view chores assigned to family members
  • Can view monthly budgets
  • Can view category budgets
  • Can create expenses

    • First, create an account. Then, you can view all the chores assigned to you, check the family budget and make spendings according to the budgets.
